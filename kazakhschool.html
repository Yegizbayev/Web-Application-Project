Kazakh Language Learning Center

In order to foster the language learning opportunities for the children of the Kazakhstani community, Shanyraq Kazakh Foundation organizes the Kazakh Language Learning Center for the Children in the City of Houston.

The objective of this center is to create a creative and resourceful environment for the children to learn and expand Kazakh language proficiency as well as to be exposed to the rich cultural and musical heritage of the Republic of Kazakhstan.

The Kazakh Language, national dance and dombyra classes in the Kazakh Language Learning Center for the Children is planned to be carried during the weekends with half-day educational program.
